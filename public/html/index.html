About IControl

At IControl we know and understand small business. Often times software is designed for larger companies with hugh inventories and often sold as being benefical and applicable to smaller entities. Time for the small business owner is of the essence and dedicating time to learning complex software that was not been designed with small business in mind can simply be a waste of time. The staff here at IControl understand the benefits of inventory managment for large companies. We wanted to create a simplifed Inventory Management System that could integrate with a Point of Sale System, specifically for the small business owner. Easy to integrate into a small business, perfect for planning and expanding.
